Introduction to Favitude

Favitude is your one stop destination for favicon generation, as over 200 favicons are generated daily using our platform. These favicons can be generated from text, uploaded images or emojis and downloaded in a specific resolution or in a zip format containing standard resolutions. We give users access to a library of pre-generated favicons and offer the option for the favicons to be edited based on colors, letters or keywords before being downloaded.

Favitude is user-friendly and straightforward and it allows you to generate or create a custom icon within minutes. Our platform offers a preview option allowing you see how your favicon will look on your website. Our generator will create the different size icons automatically and will give you the HTML code too. Our generator creates both the .ico, .png and the HTML code needed to make your site awesome looking on every browser and every device.

How to generate a favicon

#1 Image generate

You'll have to start with a picture that embodies your brand, then. It should be straightforward to identify in a busy toolbar, easy to remember, and feature the colors and distinctive "look and feel" of your company. Your logo, or at least a condensed version of it, is a wonderful place to start for all the above reasons plus more.

Step 1:

Click on generate favicon from the home page

Step 2:

Click on Image generator

Step 3:

Choose image

Step 4:

Click generate favicon button

#2 Text generate

You don’t have a logo or image for your website or brand and would like to generate one from scratch, the favitude generate from text feature is the one for you!. It is created ny using several letters or only one letter which has a striking appeal on your website leaving it simple and unique.

Step 1:

Click on generate favicon from the home page

Step 2:

Click on Text generator

Step 3:

Enter prefered text, font family, font size, background shape, font color and background color.

Step 4:

Click generate favicon button

#3 How to add your favicon to your website

To make sure that browsers can find your favicon file and show the image appropriately, you must then upload your favicon (in.ico format) to your site's root directory.

Step 1: Name Your Favicon

Make sure that your favicon is named favicon.ico. The naming of the file is important because web browsers automatically look for a file with that specific name.

Step 2: Upload Your Favicon

Upload your favicon to your website's main or root directory. The file should be accessible at your website.com/favicon.ico. The location of the file is important because web browsers will automatically look for a file named favicon.ico in that specific location.
